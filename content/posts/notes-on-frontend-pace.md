# Notes on Frontend Pace

Teams usually slow down for the same reason products do: too many one-off decisions.

If the structure is obvious, shipping becomes routine. If every screen starts with reinvention, progress becomes expensive.

## Prefer repeatable moves

Choose a few layout patterns, a few typography scales, and a few interaction patterns. Repeat them until they feel inevitable.

## Keep the system humane

The point of structure is not rigidity. The point is to lower the cost of making good decisions.
