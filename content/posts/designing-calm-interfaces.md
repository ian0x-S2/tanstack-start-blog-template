# Designing Calm Interfaces

Minimal does not mean empty. It means every element earns its place.

The fastest way to make a product feel elegant is to reduce the amount of explanation a user needs before they can move. Clear hierarchy, stable spacing, and restrained color do more work than novelty.

## A useful constraint

Give each screen one primary action and one supporting path. When every element competes for attention, nothing feels intentional.

## The payoff

Calm interfaces age well. They are easier to extend, easier to scan, and easier to trust.
